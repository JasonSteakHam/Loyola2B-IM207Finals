About ShippyDash

ShippyDash is a modern ferry service committed to transforming sea travel across the Philippine islands. Built on the pillars of safety, speed, and convenience, we offer a seamless booking experience, reliable schedules, and comfortable vessels designed for both passengers and cargo. Whether you're commuting between provinces or planning a coastal getaway, ShippyDash ensures your journey is smooth, secure, and on time.

Our mission is to connect communities and boost regional mobility by setting a new standard in ferry transportation. With a growing fleet and expanding routes, we're proud to serve as a trusted link between island destinations — empowering tourism, trade, and everyday travel.

At ShippyDash, we don't just move people — we move possibilities. FerrySafe. FerryFast. FerryConvenient.
